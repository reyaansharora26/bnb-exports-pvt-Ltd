About BNB Export

B. N. Exports Private Limited, was formed in 2007 and is owned and controlled by the second generation of a famous Rajasthani business family with over three decades of hands-on experience with sesame. This is a major Indian intermediary food company manufacturing edible oil, specializing in Sesame in different variants. The company is accredited as a Govt. of India – Star Export House and certified by ISO 9001:2015 & 22000:2005, GMP, HACCP, HALAL, Kosher, FDA (Register Corp. - USA) and FSSAI - Government of India - Food Safety & Standard Authority.

In addition, it is also a member of statutory bodies and export councils – Federation of Indian Export Organization (FIEO), Indian Oilseeds & Produce Export Promotion Council (IOPEPC) & Shellac and Forest Products Export Promotion Council (SHEFEXIL).

The company's factory is located in Domjur, an hour’s drive from Kolkata – Netaji S.C.B. International Airport. The factory is spread over 6 acres with the most updated production facilities. The ‘crushing unit’ comprises imported modern machinery. Our crushing is ‘cold press’ with ‘one-single press’ pass only. Further, the mini oil refining unit is a combination of Taiwanese and European technology producing 50 tons per day capacity. The refinery uses ‘German activated carbon’ and chemicals in our refining process. The company credits itself for having an advanced in-house, hi-tech lab comprising highly experienced food technologists. The lab checks our seeds and oil at input & output of every stage to maintain superior quality. The factory’s total built-up area comprising the factory, warehouse, office building, workers' area, etc. is in excess of 110,000 sq.ft (11,000 sq.mt), used for seed storage and equipped with large silos for oil storage.

B. N. Exports Private Limited is a market leader in sourcing and production of Sesame Oil, not just in India but in the Indian sub-continent because it specializes in Sesame. We manufacture different types of sesame oil of superior quality:

Our sesame oil is tested for 372 types of chemicals and is free of heavy metal. Test reports are always available for every production lot. The company can arrange to send samples for further testing for chemical residue.

The sesame oil in bulk is exported in British origin, special 5-layered flexi tank containing 21.3 tons to various countries around the world. We also export worldwide in retail packaging (500 ml, 1 lt, 2 Lts, 5 lts & 20 lts) under our house brand – BNB. Products are shipped around the world to Mexico, USA, Canada, The Netherlands, Austria, Germany, Iran, UAE, Vietnam, China, Taiwan, Malaysia, Singapore, etc. Private label is also in our product line.

Our international presence is found at all major exhibitions i.e. ANUGA, SIAL, GULFOOD, FANCY FOOD (Summer), and some regional / country-specific food exhibitions.
